What We Stand For

Our Story.

Get Kinder is a registered nonprofit dedicated to using technology for social good. Inspired by the simple question: What if kindness was contagious? We use Kinder AI™ and innovative AI for Good strategies to make it easy to start kindness habits and share, celebrate, and inspire kindness in others.

We use technology as a force for good to help create meaningful connections, support emotional well-being, and reduce loneliness. We ensure that every act of kindness contributes to a more just, connected, and sustainable world.

Mission.

At Get Kinder our mission is to make kindness effortless and impactful, transforming individual actions into global change making the world kinder and happier. Kinder AI™ supports people in creating daily kindness habits that improve emotional well-being, build community connections, and align with the United Nations’ 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

We do this by making it easy to be kind, as this doesn’t just brighten someone else’s day — it transforms yours!

Vision.

We envision a kinder, more compassionate world driven by innovative AI for Good solutions. By harnessing Kinder AI™ and aligning with the UN's 17 Sustainable Development Goals, we aim to foster resilience, strengthen communities, and amplify global happiness—one intentional act of kindness at a time.

Get Kinder, doing good feels good.
