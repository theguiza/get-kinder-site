Get Kinder Accessability Policy

At Get Kinder AI, an initiative of the Get Kindr Foundation, we believe that kindness and inclusion go hand in hand. We are committed to making our digital platforms accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities.

Our Commitment

We strive to meet or exceed the following accessibility standards:

  • WCAG 2.1 Level AA
  • Accessible Canada Act (ACA)
  • British Columbia Societies Act Principles on inclusion and non-discrimination

Key Features We Aim to Provide

We are working to ensure that our website and mobile application are designed to be accessible to all users, including:

  • Text alternatives for non-text content
  • Keyboard-friendly navigation
  • Screen reader compatibility
  • Color contrast and text resizing options
  • Text alternatives for all images and media
  • Mobile-optimized, accessible design

Known Limitations

We acknowledge that some third-party tools or embedded services (e.g., forms, external platforms) may not yet fully meet these standards. We are committed to working with vendors to address and resolve these issues wherever possible.

Request Assistance or Provide Feedback

If you encounter any barriers while using our site or require content in a different format, we welcome your feedback. We are committed to responding and making reasonable accommodations where possible.
